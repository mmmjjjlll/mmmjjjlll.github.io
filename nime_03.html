A Note about Hugh Le Caine

One of the most rewarding talks at NIME 03 was the retrospective on Hugh Le Caine given by Alcides Lanza. Particularly important for us is the emphasis Hugh Le Caine placed on the playability and expressivity of the instruments he built. I suspect there is still much to be learned from Le Caine's work. A good starting point for the Le Caine story is this article by Gayle Young. Also see the Hugh Le Caine site. I highly recommend the CD Hugh Le Caine: Compositions & Demonstrations 1946-1974, available from the Electronic Music Foundation (also on amazon.com).
